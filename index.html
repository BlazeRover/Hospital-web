Dr.Marco Bianchi

Heart disease: In the field of Cardiology, doctors focus on the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and disorders affecting the heart and blood vessels. Dr. Marco is one of the most experienced doctors in caring for patients with diseases such as hypertension and coronary artery disease. He can also do examinations such as electrocardiography and radiography to assess heart health.

Dr.Elena Petrova

Neuroscience: In the field of neurology, doctors specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the central and peripheral nervous system. Dr. Elena is considered one of the most competent doctors as she treats conditions such as migraines, cerebral palsy and multiple sclerosis. She can also perform neurological functional tests to assess nerve function and provide appropriate treatments such as drug therapy and physiotherapy.
